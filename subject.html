Description

League of Legends is a 5v5 multiplayer online video game. There are more than 150 playable characters to choose from that all have their own unique abilities. League of Legends promotes strategy and quick thinking. Each team will have 5 roles that the players choose before the match starts. The goal of the game is to work with your team to destroy the opponents base. The team that is first to do this wins.

    • Benefits

    League of Legends requires teamwork and synergy. This means that playing with friends is the best way to play. Gathering a group of 5 and queuing up can serve as a fun way to spend time with friends.

    • Downsides

    Just like any other competitive video game, addictions can easily be formed. League of Legends is no exception to this. Many players end up getting caught up in League of Legend's ranked system, spending hours each day playing. As a result, things like sleep loss, lack of exercise, and decreasing quality of diet may occur.
