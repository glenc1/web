Instructions

  1. To start, press run.
  2. Start typing. Do not begin with a space.
  3. Type as many words as you can.
  4. You do not need to include commas. Just type out the words seperated by spaces.
  5. When you have typed all of the words, press enter.
  6. If you would like to play again, type "y" when asked if you would like to play again
  7. If you do not want to continue, type "n"
  8. After typing "n" you will recieve a graph showcasing all of your scores.
