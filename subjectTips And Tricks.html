League of Legends is a very complicated game. For new players learning the game may seem very daunting. Provided here are some tips and tricks that could be useful to know!

- When you are starting out picking a champion can be hard. Instead of basing your choice off of playstyle, just go for a champion that you thinks looks cool

- It is never worth it to flame your teamates in chat. It wont increase your chances of winning. On the other hand, giving compliments are sending a nice gesture their way can help a ton.

- Every match is winnable. Try your best and dont give up.

- When Zed uses ult on you, he will always appear behind you.

- Playing ARAM or ARURF can be a good way to try new champions that you would not have tried otherwise.

- Utilize practice tool as much as possible. Its great for learning a new champion.

- The best time to recall in lane is when the next incoming minion wave has a cannon minion in it.

- Always try your best to chain your CC. This will make getting kills a lot easier.

- If you want to improve your last hitting skills, go to practice tool and pick a range champion with very low attack damage, such as Soraka. Try to last hit as many minions as you can. Over time you will definately start to see some improvment.

- Muting your teamates is one of the best things you can do to increase your chances of winning. Most of the time they have nothing imoportant to say.

- Taking a break is always a good thing. You are more likely to play worse when you are tired.
